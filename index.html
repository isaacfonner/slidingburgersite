Parkour Spiral:

A Massive parkour map, three times the size of parkour spiral but in a similar form factor, stretches all the way from bedrock to build height. Make your way threw 30+ different levels, to reach the top.

Commands:

/pa join spiral:
Starts the Parkour (or click the end crystal)
/pa restart:
Restarts the parkour course
/pa back:
Teleports to last checkpoint
/pa info {user}:
View parkour information on a user

Bedwars:

Explore 10+ Bedwars maps, with 15 different gamemodes, including special ones such as rush mode, and 8 team ultimate mode.

Duels and FFA:

Enjoy Free for all brawls in a massive arena, with 12 different kits for you to use, or play 1v1 duels, with 8 kits, including Crystal, Sword, Bow, NoDebuff, and more!

Commands:

/duel {user}:
Opens a GUI to send a duel request to a user
/queue:
Opens a GUI to join different duels queues
/ua join pvp:
Teleports to last checkpoint
/pa info {user}:
View parkour information on a user

Survival:

Cheeseburger Network strives to provide a reliable and fun survival experience, with all the useful commands you are used to, plus some unique ones, and a amazing economy

Commands:

/rtp:
TPs player to a random location within 3000 blocks of spawn
/tpa {username}:
Sends a request to a user; if accepted TPs to them
/sethome {name}:
Saves a location to tp to later; use /home {name} to go back
/spawn:
Teleports the user to spawn.
/shop:
Opens the shop gui, to buy or sell any item
/sellgui:
Opens a empty gui were you can drop any items to sell them
/claim:
Claims the area around you; use /trust to share your claim
/bal:
Shows your money; Use /pay to send money
/trade:
Sends a request to trade with someone, opens a trade gui on accept
